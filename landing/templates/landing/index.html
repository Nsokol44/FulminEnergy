Our Mission
Energy On Demand

Clean power from where you want it and when you need it. Our goal is to allow you to optimize your energy use by renting PowerCells that are charged with the energy of your choosing. Utilize the best energy source for your needs.


How We Work
Not Tied to the Grid

Fulmin utilizes portable battery technology and flexible charging solutions for those batteries. There is no need to purchase and install an expensive battery or solar panel solution for your home. Merely download our app, install our FulPlug, and start renting batteries charged by the cleanest energy sources. Our PowerOrb technology will be delivered to your home on the same day and all you have to do is plug it in to get power for your devices.


1. Log into our mobile/web app.
2. Select your power source.
3. Have a PowerCell delivered to your home. Simply notify us when you need another or want to return it until your next use.
